About the Park

This Harlem community park features an open lawn, terraced stone seating, perennial gardens, and a paved plaza for art installations, markets, festivals, and performances.

Neighborhood Association

The Montefiore Park Neighborhood Association assists NYC Parks with maintenance, gardening, community involvement, and event coordination.

2021 Redesign

The Association played a key role in advocating for the 2021 redesign, shaping a more flexible and community‑focused public space.
